Privacy Policy

1. General Information

ThinkyTots was created during the Minihackathon 2.0 by Kevin Chromik. Our platform is designed to promote playful learning for children. Protecting your data and your children's privacy is our top priority.

2. Data Collection and Storage

ThinkyTots does not collect, store, or process any personal data. We are committed to providing a safe and secure learning environment for children without compromising their privacy.
